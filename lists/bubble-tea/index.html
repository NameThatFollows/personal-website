    Fantastic

    Really tasty bubble tea and/or unique concepts. If I could only go to once place, it'd be one of these

    Brew Cha

    3420 18th St #001, San Francisco, CA 94110

    Delicious teas and interesting specialty drinks. One of the few places that sells a Ube Taro Latte! Unrelated, but it also has super fast free Wi-Fi.

    Plentea

    341 Kearny St, San Francisco, CA 94108

    Rich and generally delicious bubble tea, and they have a glass bottle option. They also have a non-powder taro milk tea and other interesting specialty drinks.

    Black Sugar - Boba Bar

    320 O'Farrell St, San Francisco, CA 94102

    Small location with fairly limited options, but the drinks they offer are delicious, especially the Mango Matcha Latte and the Roasted Oolong Milk Tea

    Teaspoon

    2361 Broadway, Redwood City, CA 94063

    Huge variety of different teas, super aesthetic three-layer drinks, and fantastic shaved ice as a bonus. Located just blocks away from Redwood City Caltrain and Courthouse Square.

    Tea & Milk

    32-02 34th Avenue, Astoria, NY 11106, US
